Plum — Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Plum does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

How It Works

The extension processes web page content entirely within your browser to generate a reader view. Page HTML is temporarily held in browser session storage during an active reading session and is deleted immediately after use. No data is sent to external servers.

External Resources

The extension loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) to render the reader view. Google's privacy policy applies to those requests.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact privacy@matijao.com.
